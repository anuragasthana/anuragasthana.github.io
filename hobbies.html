I have several hobbies like playing video games, programming on computers, building and programming robots, music and reading.

Playing video games is one of my favorite pastimes. My favorite video game currently is "Halo: The master chief collection". Check out the video games section in my website.

Robotics is another of my favorite hobbies. Growing up I loved playing with Legos. I was really good at building complicated stuff. When I was in elementary school, I was introduced to building and programming robots using the Lego MindStorm kit. Programming the robot was really fun and was my first step into the world of programming. Programming these robots was my first introduction to the FLL competitions. The theme for 2012 competition was "Senior Solutions". In 2011 the theme was "Food Contamination". Participants work in a team and build robots and execute missions as per the instructions outlined in the competition. Additionally teams are supposed to execute a project in an area pertaining to the competition theme. I have enjoyed these competition very much. Thes fun competitions inspired me to participate in the FLL for four years.

I also love to listen to music. I listen to modern and classic rock, as well as a little bit of rap and soft rock. I've also learned to play the piano and have participated in many recitals. Check out my recital videos.

Growing up I also loved to play Ping Pong (or Table Tennis). I learned to play the game as a fun game with my family. Later as I got better at it my parents introduced me to ping pong coach who participated in the Olympics! Check out the pictures of me playing.

Since sixth grade, I have been a part of Boy Scouts. I am currently a Life Scout in Troop 290 and have had many amazing experiences with my troop. Whether it be camping, hiking, or running a troop meeting, Boy Scouts has always been something that I love to be a part of.

Another hobby of mine is watching movies. "Star Wars" has been my favorite movie series ever since it was introduced to me by a friend at the age of five. I am always interested in "Star Wars" lore and theories about any future movies. I also love to watch superhero movies like "The Avengers" and "Guardians of the Galaxy".

I also love to read books in my spare time. I prefer to read fiction novels.Some of my favorite series include "Harry Potter" by J.K. Rowling, "The Secrets of the Immortal Nicholas Flamel" by Michael Scott, "The Heroes of Olympus" by Rick Riordan, the "Inheritance" series by Christopher Paolini, "The Mysterious Benedict Society" by Trenton Lee Stewart and “The Lord of The Rings” by JRR Tolkien.
