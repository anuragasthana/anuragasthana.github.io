Video games have been a part of my life ever since I was a child. Although there are many other games that
I like to play on different consoles, here are my 4 favorites.

#1

With 4 extensive stories, lots of action, and a knack
for heroism, "Halo: The Master Chief Collection" is my favorite
video game because it shows the story of Spartan John 117 in the
distant future as he fights to save humanity and discover who he is.

#2

A struggle between 2 groups of Spartans in the distant
future proves to lead to their own destruction. "Halo 5: Guardians"
lands at #2 because of the interesting story and the introduction
of new multiplayer modes that bring more action and more fun.

#3

Ever since I was a child, I was always
fascinated by "Lego" video games because they always had many
different different puzzles to solve. They also made me think of
creative ways to get out of a situation and finish the level.
"Lego: The Hobbit" is no exception to this.

#4

The thrill of racing can never get old in
"Forza Horizon 2". While racing through mountains, highways, and
small towns as my circuits, it was easy to forget that it was
just a video game.
