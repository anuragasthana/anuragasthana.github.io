I have several hobbies like playing video games, programming on computers, building and programming robots, music and reading.

I like playing video games whenever I find time. Video games have always been a part of my hobby. I enjoy being a part of the adventure and action of the game.My favorite video game currently is "Halo:The master chief collection". Checkout the video games section in my website.

I have been around computers from a very young age. I have always enjoyed seeing my programming creation come to life on a screen. I can currently program in Java, HTML and Javascript. I hope to learn many more programming languages and build some amazing programs in future.I also wish that in the future I will get a job as a computer scientist.

Robotics is another of my favorite hobbies. Growing up I loved playing with Legos. I was really good at building complicated stuff. My dad later introduced me to building and programming robots using the Lego Mindstorm kit. Programming the robot was really fun and was my first step into the world of programming.
First Lego League is an organization that hosts a Robotics competition in the United States. I have participated in this Robotics competition from 3rd to 7th grade. It is really a fun and educational event. The competition is based on a theme. The theme for 2012 competition was "Senior Solutions". In 2011 the theme was "Food Contamination". Participants work in a team and build robots and execute missions as per the instructions outlined in the competition. Additionally teams are supposed to execute a project in an area pertaining to the competition theme. I have enjoyed these competition very much. See the pictures of our robot on this site. Currently I am a member "Robotics Club" in my school

I also love to listen to music. I listen to modern and classic rock, as well as a little bit of rap and pop music. I've also learned to play the piano and have participated in many recitals. Check out my recital videos.

Growing up I also loved to play Ping Pong (or Table Tennis). I learned to play the game as a fun game with my family. Later as I got better at it my parents introduced me to ping pong coach!! Check out the pictures of me playing.

Since sixth grade, I have been a part of Boy Scouts. I am currently a Life Scout in Troop 290 and have had many amazing experiences with my troop. Whether it be camping, hiking, or running a troop meeting, Boy Scouts has always been something that I love to be a part of.

I love all of the movies - except for the prequels :D - and I am always interested in "Star Wars" lore and theories about any future movies. I also love to watch superhero movies like "The Avengers" and "Guardians of the Galaxy".
